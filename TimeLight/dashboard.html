Welcome to TimeLight!

A web based (soon available on mobile) app Inspired by the complexity and mystery of our minds. In all their cosmic
complexity, our minds often tuck away memories too heavy to hold. Maybe a traumatic event you'd like to finally just to be
proud of your healing, or a happy moment you'd not want to lose. TimeLight promises to keep those precious momentbr
of life you hold dear, the memories you'd love to fly back to in time and revisit. This app is a safe constellation
for what might've been lost in the shadows. A tribute to healing, remembering, and honoring every phase of our
emotional universe.
